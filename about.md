Hi, my name is Torsten Scholak. This is my personal website.

<img src="/assets/images/medresden.jpg" alt="giving a talk at the 2011 DPG Spring Meeting in Dresden" class="full-img">

I'm a theoretical physicist in the group of [Paul Brumer](http://www.chem.utoronto.ca/ppl/faculty_profile.php?id=7) ([Chemical Physics Theory Group](http://www.chem.utoronto.ca/cptg/) and [Centre for Quantum Information and Quantum Control](http://cqiqc.physics.utoronto.ca) at the [University of Toronto](http://www.utoronto.ca), Canada). In 2011, after three years in the group of [Andreas Buchleitner](http://www.mathphys.uni-freiburg.de/physik/dozenten.php?id=60) ([Quantum Optics and Statistics Group](http://www.quantum.uni-freiburg.de)), I received my PhD in physics from the [University of Freiburg](http://www.uni-freiburg.de/universitaet-en), Germany.

You can find me on UofT’s downtown campus in the chemistry department. My office address is
[423-80 St. George St, Toronto, ON, M5S 3H6, Canada](https://www.google.ca/maps/place/Lash+Miller+Chemical+Labs,+University+of+Toronto+-+St.+George+Campus,+Toronto,+ON+M5S/@43.6616258,-79.3982564,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x882b34bf392bdf79:0x487eeffb1ccdd985).
